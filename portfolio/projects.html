  • 2. Miami Marlins Case Study for Organization Behavior of Sport Organization ---- This was a very interesting project I worked on last year. I along with three classmates broke down what has gone wrong with the Miami Marlins managment, and why this might be. In 2020, they hired the first female General Manager in the big 4 American Sports, but within three years she stepped down. Their CEO, Derek Jeter had also just left the organization. As a group we looked in to posible causes of this, while also working on a solution to fix it. To us, there is a clear issue from the top of managment, which is why we think it is best for the Marlins to completley change their sturcture. We created a new Managment tree, so everyone knows who is in charge. With this system in place no one should feel undercut, and everyone will be on the same page.
